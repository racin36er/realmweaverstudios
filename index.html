It Begins... Posted by Tim@dev
on January 3, 2024


Welcome to Realmweaver Studios! Founded in 2024, Realmweaver is a small indie game developer based in Canada. We are currently working on developing our first game, using the working title Mystic Masters, which is an innovative 2D MMORPG in which players must master myth and commerce, balance divine powers, build up their city, practice economic strategies and more to ensure the survival of their realm.

The game is currently in the paper phase, as we complete initial designs and finish ironing out the idea. The intention is to live stream development of the game on Twitch, which we will begin doing once coding has begun. In the mean time, feel free to check us out on Discord. You can also support the studio on Patreon.

Thanks for visiting Realmweaver Studios, be sure to subscribe on Twitch and Youtube for more content in the coming months.
